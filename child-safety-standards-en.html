🛡️ Child Safety Standards

QuickChat

QuickChat is a utility tool to open WhatsApp directly without saving phone numbers. The app does not provide any chat or direct communication features between users.

🛡️ Child Protection Policy

1. Nature of the Application

QuickChat is a Utility Tool, not a social networking platform:

  • ✓ No User-Generated Content: The app does not allow creating or sharing any content
  • ✓ No Chat Features: The app only opens external WhatsApp
  • ✓ No User Interaction: Users cannot communicate with each other within the app
  • ✓ No Cloud Storage: All data is stored locally on the user's device
  • ✓ No User Profiles: No accounts or profiles can be created

2. App's Only Function

QuickChat does:

  1. Allow users to enter a phone number
  2. Open the official WhatsApp app installed on the device
  3. Save a local history of used numbers (on device only)
⚠️ Important: The app does not control any content exchanged via WhatsApp. WhatsApp is fully responsible for its own security and privacy policies.

📋 QuickChat's Commitments

1. Not Targeting Children

  • The app is not directed at children under 13 years old
  • The app does not collect any personal data from users
  • Age rating: Everyone - general utility tool

2. No Reportable Content

Since the app:

  • Does not contain chat or communication features
  • Does not allow uploading or sharing content
  • Has no user interaction

No reporting mechanism is required, as the app does not produce or host any content.

3. Legal Compliance

QuickChat complies with all local and international laws related to child protection, including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) laws

🔒 Privacy and Security Policy

Data Stored Locally Only:

  • Phone numbers (stored locally on device)
  • Message templates (stored locally on device)
  • App settings (stored locally on device)

Not Collected:

  • ✗ Personal information
  • ✗ Location data
  • ✗ Photos or media files
  • ✗ Contacts
  • ✗ Chat messages
